Evacuation plan $ safety procedure

In case of emergency, follow this evacuation plan!

Follow this strategies:

  1. Leave the building through the nearest exit.
  2. Leave immediately, do not return for personal things.
  3. If possible, keep others informed about the evacuation.
  4. Help others evacuate where possible.
  5. Evacuate no matter if others agree to follow.
  6. Use the safest possible evacuation route.
  7. Avoids hazard contact (fire, suspicious package, violent intruder, etc.).
  8. Prevents people from entering an area where the hazard may be present and Don't use lifts.

Important notes:

  • Before an emergency, it is important to be aware of nearest exits and build emergency evacuation routes.
  • Take note of primary and secondary escape routes to ensure that you are prepared to act quickly and ensure your safety if anything occurs.
